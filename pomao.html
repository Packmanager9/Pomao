Pomao's Quest!

  • WAD to Steer
  • Eat the fruit
  • Use IJKL/Arrow keys to direct tongue and eggs
  • Spacebar to lick, M to throw egg
  • N or F to ground-pound while jumping
  • Plugged in controllers are supported
  • For controllers its twinstick, X to ground pound, and RT+LS to shoot eggs
  • Down on right stick to go through thin platforms
