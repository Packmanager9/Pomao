Pomao's Quest!

  • E/right-trigger to talk to characters
  • WAD to Steer
  • Eat the fruit
  • Use IJKL/Arrow keys to direct tongue and eggs
  • Spacebar to lick, M to throw egg
  • N or F to ground-pound while jumping
  • P or start to pause/unpause
  • Plugged in controllers are supported
  • For controllers its twinstick, X to ground pound, and RT+RS to shoot eggs
  • Down on left stick to go through thin platforms
  • S to go through thin platforms
